About Me

I am an integrative psychotherapist and counsellor offering both short-term and longer-term therapy. Being an integrative therapist means I draw on a range of different therapeutic approaches, rather than working from one single model. This allows me to tailor the work to each individual, their needs, and what feels most helpful at different stages of therapy.

My work is relational at its core, meaning I place importance on the therapeutic relationship, as this is widely recognised as an important part of the healing process. I support clients in gently exploring how past experiences may still be showing up in their present.

I specialise in relational trauma and childhood trauma, with a particular focus on attachment and early relational experiences. My work is also informed by personal experience alongside professional training.

I also support parents navigating emotional pressures and people experiencing anxiety, depression, overwhelm, low self-worth, and feeling stuck.

I aim to offer a calm, non-judgemental space where you can feel heard and supported at your own pace.

I work online via video and telephone sessions.

Getting in touch

If you would like to enquire about starting therapy, please email me with a brief outline of what you are looking for support with.

I will then arrange a 15-minute introductory telephone call to explore whether working together feels like a good fit.

If we decide to proceed, I will send initial paperwork including a contract and intake forms.

We will then arrange a full 50-minute initial consultation session.
