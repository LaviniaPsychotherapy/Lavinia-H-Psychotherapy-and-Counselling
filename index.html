About Me

I am an integrative psychotherapist and counsellor offering both short-term and longer-term therapy. I draw on a range of therapeutic approaches to tailor the work to each individual.

My work is relational at its core, with a focus on the therapeutic relationship as part of the healing process.

I specialise in relational trauma, childhood trauma, attachment, anxiety, depression, overwhelm, and low self-worth.

I aim to offer a calm, non-judgemental space where you can feel heard and supported at your own pace.

I work online via video and telephone.

Fees

My standard fee is £60 per 50-minute session.

I offer a limited number of concessionary spaces for those on lower incomes, students, care leavers, or those receiving benefits.

Please mention this in your enquiry if needed.

Getting in touch

If you would like to enquire about starting therapy, please email me with a brief outline of what you are looking for support with.

I will then arrange a 15-minute introductory telephone call to explore whether working together feels like a good fit.

If we decide to proceed, I will send initial paperwork including a contract and intake forms.

We will then arrange a full 50-minute initial consultation session.
